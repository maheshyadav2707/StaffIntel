# StaffIntel Vision

## Mission

Help US IT staffing companies identify the 20 companies most likely to need staffing help every day.

Instead of spending hours searching LinkedIn, business development teams should start their day with a ranked list of opportunities, understand why those companies were selected, find the right decision makers, and generate personalized outreach in minutes.

---

## Success

A user opens StaffIntel every morning instead of LinkedIn.

StaffIntel tells them:

- Who to contact
- Why they should contact them
- Who inside the company
- What message to send

The user spends their day having conversations instead of searching.

---

## Version 1 Goals

- Show Top 20 companies
- Opportunity Score
- Response Probability
- Confidence Score
- Decision Makers
- AI-generated Email
- AI-generated LinkedIn Message
